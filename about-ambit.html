ABOUT AMBIT

Ambition Leading Progress

AMBIT is not just a semiconductor design and intellectual property design services company; it's a bold and visionary venture led by a dynamic team of young entrepreneurs. Driven by an unwavering passion to explore uncharted territories in the world of semiconductor design, the founders have not only cultivated successful partnerships with esteemed clients but have also become trailblazers in mastering new and emerging technologies within the semiconductor design arena.


The AMBIT Advantage

AMBIT is a trusted partner recognized by valued clients from various corners of the world. We are the steadfast cornerstone in the journey of your product's lifecycle, engaging with you right from the conceptual stage and standing by your side throughout the arduous path to testing and production. Our reputation as a reliable and high-quality partner precedes us, ensuring you have comprehensive support every step of the way. When you choose AMBIT, you choose excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to your success.

Vision

To explore new frontiers in semiconductor design and to be the partner of choice for our clients in the world a better place.

Mission

  • Encourage innovation and lead the team to new avenues.
  • Offer invaluable service that always keeps us a cut above the competition.
  • Develop an ecosystem of documented knowledge in semiconductor design to further R&D.
