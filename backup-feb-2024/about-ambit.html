ABOUT AMBIT

It is through innovation and value addition that a company distinguishes itself from the competition. Ambit is one such company that believes in this adage and works towards delivering better services to our clients.

Ambit offers semiconductor design and intellectual property design services to global clients and adopts Offshore Design Centre (ODC) approach in achieving this. ODC functions like an extended arm of the client and thus ensures close communication and coordination between the client's teams and ambitions.

The founders' passion to innovate in this domain has led them to achieve many successful partnerships with valued clients and has also helped in mastering new and emerging technologies in semiconductor design space.
