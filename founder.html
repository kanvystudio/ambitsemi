AMBIT Founder's Visionary Insight

“The whole world of electronics was changing, and I just had to get on the ground floor to build something great.”

- J Saseendra /Founder & CEO of AMBIT

With an impressive 23-year journey in the dynamic world of semiconductor industries, Saseendra has truly been a trailblazer. His remarkable career has seen him collaborating with industry giants like DRDO, Chartered Semiconductors, AMD, Synopsys and Qualcomm, leaving a significant mark at each step. Saseendra's career began in the heart of fabrication, where he delved into the intricacies of Si, GaAs, and MEMs technologies, mastering their unique challenges and possibilities. He then transitioned into the world of design, leaving a trail of successful Tape Outs spanning from 180nm to the cutting-edge 3nm technologies in ASIC’s/MMIC’s. A consistent leader, the driving force behind countless innovations, with Physical Design as his core expertise.

His educational journey mirrors his professional success, as he proudly holds a Master's degree in Electronics from the prestigious University of Hyderabad. Saseendra's career is a testament to the limitless possibilities one can achieve through dedication, passion, and a relentless pursuit of excellence in the ever-evolving semiconductor industry.
