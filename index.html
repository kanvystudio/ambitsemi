Trusted Partner in Semiconductor Engineering

AMBIT, based in Bengaluru, is a leading semiconductor design and intellectual property company founded by young, driven entrepreneurs. Our comprehensive semiconductor design services, including ASIC Design, Verification, Physical Design Implementation, DFT, Timing Analysis, Circuit Design & Layout, FPGA, Foundation IP design, PSV, and Emulation, cater to the entire semiconductor design process, from concept to final products. Our seasoned team of engineers has a proven track record of delivering ASIC designs in advanced technology nodes, with expertise extending to 3nm.

AMBIT's Cutting-Edge Design and IP Services

Product Design and Development

AMBIT excels in ASIC, Embedded Devices, and Product Development for sectors such as Medical Electronics and Agriculture, translating ideas into tangible products.

VLSI Design

Ambit’s VLSI design services cover RTL coding to tape out, specializing in signoff checks and timing closure for meticulous precision.

Automation

AMBIT provides comprehensive automation solutions, from flow and methodology bring-up to validation and regression runs across technology nodes and design architectures, accelerating development processes.

Semiconductor Manufacturing

AMBIT offers extensive support in VLSI fabrication, guiding clients from cleanroom architecture to flow and process technology bring-up for seamless semiconductor manufacturing integration.
