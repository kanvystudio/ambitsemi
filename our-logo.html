OUR LOGO  

The logo resembles the English alphabet ‘a’ as well as the infinity symbol. It denotes the infinite scope for possibilities. Innovations in the field of semiconductor design are infinite and there is a huge scope that still waiting to be tapped. While engineers and scientists world over are working at nano levels, there is much more that can be done in semiconductor design that can further help in improving the quality of human life on this planet.


The tagline “innovation and beyond” too reinforces this philosophy.


The typography and colours give a modern looking feel – thus indicating that the majority of the workforce at Ambit are from the younger generations with full of talent, vigour and enthusiasm, raring to go and make a mark for themselves.
